Our Process

Four Steps.
Zero Shortcuts.

01

Assess

We photograph every angle, document every dent, and build your repair plan. You see exactly what we see — no surprises, no hidden fees. Insurance estimates handled same-day.

02

Prep

Surface prep is 70% of a perfect finish. We strip, sand, prime, and block until the surface is glass. Metal work, rust repair, and panel alignment happen here — before any paint touches the car.

03

Paint

Color-matched with a spectrophotometer. Applied in our climate-controlled downdraft booth. Base coat, clear coat, and wet sanding for a finish that's deeper than factory.

04

Reveal

Buffed, polished, and detailed. We walk you around the car, point-by-point. If you can find the repair, we'll redo it. That's the DG standard.
